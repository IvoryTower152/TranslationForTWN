#### 句式

- Philip Lombard, summing up the girl opposite in a mere flash of his quick moving eyes thought to himself: "Quite attractive - a bit schoolmistressy perhaps."

  菲利普·伦巴第，用他快速移动的眼光打量了一番坐在对面的女性，想到：“相当迷人，也许是一个女教师.”

- It was as though he knew very well that in Lombard's past actions legality had not always been a sine qua non.

  他似乎很清楚，在伦巴第过去的行为中，合法性并不总是一个必要条件。
