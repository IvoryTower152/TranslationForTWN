### 翻译训练 Chapter_1_02

#### 句子

- Various glowing advertisements of it had appeared in the papers.

  报纸上刊登了越来越多关于它出售的广告。

- Then came the first bald statement that it had been bought by a Mr. Owen. After that the numerous of gossip writers had started. Indian Island had really been bought by Miss Gabrielle Turl, the Hollywood film star! She wanted to spend some months there free from all publicity!

  那之后发出了第一份声明，表示这座岛已经被欧文先生买下了。而那之后无数的八卦作家开始散布流言蜚语。有人说印第安岛实际上是被加布里埃勒·特尔小姐，也就是那位好莱坞的电影明星买下的，她想要在那里过几个月，而不引起任何注意。

- Busy Bee had hinted delicately that it was to be an abode for Royalty??! Mr. Merryweather had had it whispered to him that it had been bought for a honeymoon-Young Lord L-had surrendered to Cupid at last! Jonas knew for a fact that it had been purchased by the Admiralty with a view to carrying out some very hush hush experiments. 

  “忙碌蜜蜂”微妙地暗示它将成为皇室的住所？？！因为梅里韦瑟先生曾经悄悄地告诉他印地安岛将会被买下用于度蜜月，年轻的领主L终于向丘比特投降了(应该是收获了爱情的意思)！乔纳斯则知晓真相，这个小岛被海军部买下，目的是进行一些非常秘密的实验。
