#### 句型

- Illuminated by the setting sun, they had their first glimpse of Indian Island jutting up out of the sea to the south.

  在阳光的照耀下，他们第一次瞥见向南凸出海面的印第安岛。

- She had pictured it differently, close to shore, crowned with a beautiful white house. But there was no house visible, only the boldly silhouetted rock with its faint resemblance to a giant Indian's head.

  这与她想象的有所不同，在她的想象中，它应该临近海岸，在它的上面有一座美丽的白色房屋。然而这里看不到房子，只有轮廓粗犷的岩石，就像是印第安巨人的头一样。
